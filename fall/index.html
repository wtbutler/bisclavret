The cool air, once again dry, lifted his spirits once again. This was the season of dying. The dying of the trees, of plants. The beginning of the winter slumber for many prey. The last chance for any variety in his meals. It was soon coming up on a year of him living like this. He wasn't sure of the day. Individual days were hard to track, and there was no point anyway. It marked the dying of his hope, as well. His hope of returning to humanity. Perhaps he should move on from here. Leave this behind and try to start fresh. He could hear howling in the distance, the howling of a hunter. Of a hound.
