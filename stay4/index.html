The dogs herded him back to the part of the woods near the city. He was terrified of what would be at the end, but also ashamed. He wasn't cattle to be herded, he was the hunter! How could he have let himself be caught? But his outrage at himself couldn't last forever. He needed to escape somehow. Needed to find a way to evade his captors. He could smell humans on the breeze. It'd been so long since he'd smelled humans. He almost wanted to let them keep herding him, just so he could see a person again. Could he even escape if he tried? He was exhausted. If he was going to die, he might as well see another person once more.
